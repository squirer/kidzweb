Glock G30S

The Glock 30 in the legendary .45 caliber offers excellent out-of-the-box precision paired with the ballistic quality of a genuine big bore caliber. The smooth design and the simple operation of this Glock pistol allow a quick draw. Because of these advantages and especially because of its compact dimensions, the Glock 30 is the first choice of plainclothes officers and protection personnel.

  • .45 auto
  • 3.78" barrell
  • Capacity: 10 rounds
